Be part of the audience

Being able to express your reaction to comedian jokes with laughs for the good ones and tomatoes for the not-so-good ones makes the experience fun and engaging. It’s like having a virtual seat in the comedy club, where your opinion counts, and laughter is the currency!.

Level up your performance

Elevating your performance has never been easier than with the detailed statistics in this app. Access essential data such as age, gender, and locations of your audience, directly from the videos of your jokes. It’s like having a personalized analysis after each performance, allowing you to fine-tune your comedy to reach your audience even more impactfully.
